Story Mode

Story mode is the main mode in Blue Archive
It helps to tell every story that are happening in Kivotos

Context

Blue Archive takes place in the academy city of Kivotos. The player takes the role of a teacher, called Sensei, who is summoned by the president of the General Student Council, a committee managing the schools, before her disappearance. Criminal activity rises following her disappearance. Sensei has the task to resolve the issues around Kivotos and help find the missing president.

Story Mode

In Blue Archive the main mode is story mode which include all the story of the students in Kivotos. All of the story is differently related to different school of Kivotos. In Story mode there are also 3 different categories:
1. Main Story
2. Sub Story
3. Replay

Main Story

For now there are 3 story in the Main Story section. However there will be more coming as the game progress in the future. From here you can review the main events in Blue Archive.

Sub Story

In Sub Story you can review some of the daily lifes of the students in Kivotos. Currently there are 15 different sub story that consists of 15 different club in Kivotos.

Replay

In Replay you can relive some of the events that happens in Kivotos. There are 3 categories in Replay:
1. Relationship Story
2. Task Stories
3. Event Stories

Relationship Story

Relationship Story include all of the stories after reaching a certain point of studentsâ€™ relationship.

Task Stories

Task Stories include all of the tutorial dialogues.

Event Stories

Finally Event Stories include all of the event stories that have happen after the event had ended.
