Achievements

Daily Achievement
These are easy achievements that are available and reset daily at 04:00 JST.

Weekly Achievement
These are achievements that takes a longer time to complete and reset weekly at 04:00 JST.

Accomplishment Achievement
These are time consuming achievements that can only be completed once teacher reach a certain milestone. They can only be completed once.

Challenge Achievement
These are much more difficult and time consuming achievements that all requires teacher to clear both Normal and Hard missions under a certain turn limit. They can only be completed once.

Club

Club Is a Guild-like system in Blue Archive.

The primary feature of joining a club is that it allows a teacher to chat with other teachers. A maximum of 30 teachers can join a club. Teachers are given 10 AP everyday upon checking the Club and can be claimed at teacher's mailbox.

It can be unlocked after clearing Mission 3 Act 4.

Shop

Angel 24 or simply known as the Shop, is a place where Sensei can buy various items using the various types of currencies available in Blue Archive.

Cafe

Cafe is a place where teachers can interact with their students in Blue Archive.

Cafe allows teachers to obtain Gold, AP, and the ability to increase the Affection of their students as well. Cafe also act as a housing system allowing teachers to decorate their cafe with furnitures.

It can be unlocked after clearing Mission 2 Act 1.

Crafting Chamber

Crafting Chamber or Manufacture, is a place located within the Sanctum Tower in Kivotos that can produces various materials.

It allow teachers to produce various items such as Exp Report, Skill Material, Gift, Furniture, EX-Skill Disk and Skill Book.

It can be unlocked after clearing Mission 3 Act 2.

Lesson

Schedule or Lesson in the Global release, is a feature that allows Sensei to "check" around in both the Schale office and the several schools within Kivotos.

Lessons allow teachers to increase the Relationship of their students and earn Growth Material.

It is unlocked after completing Mission 2 Stage 4.

Affection

Bond or Relationship on Global, is a mechanic that unlocks various features in Blue Archive.

Relationship is a mechanic that allows teachers to unlock a student's Relationship Rank Bonus, Relationship Story and Memorial Lobby.

Recruit

Pickup Recruitment, or universally known as Gacha, is the gacha system in Blue Archive where Teachers are able to spend their hard-earned Pyroxenes to obtain new Students.

Similar to other gachas, players are able use their Pyroxenes to do either a single pull (120 Pyroxenes) or a ten pull (1200 Pyroxenes).
