BLUE ARCHIVE

By: Tian Jia Yi

Introduction

Blue Archive is a role-playing game developed by Nexon Games, a subsidiary of Nexon. The game is free-to-play with gacha mechanics. It was first released for Android and iOS by Shanghai Yostar in February 2021 and worldwide in November that year by Nexon. The combat system of Blue Archive features a 3D real-time environment with chibi characters.
