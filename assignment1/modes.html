Modes

Besides of story mode of Blue Archive which is the main attraction to this games, Blue Archive also has tons of exiting modes to challege the player or simply to have fun.

Mission

Mission or Campaign, are storyless campaign separated into Normal and Hard mode in Blue Archive. Missions is where is farming takes places. Normal missions drops mainly Equipment, while Hard missions drops student's Divine Fragment.

Normal Mission

Normal Mission are the main source of farming Equipment. Each stages cost 10 AP. Once the stage has been cleared and 3 star , quick clear is unlocked.

Hard Mission

Is a much more challenging stage, where student's Divine Fragment is obtained. When the stage is completed for the first time, A reward of the five Divine Fragment of the stage is distributed. Hard mode stages cost 20 AP per clear and can be cleared only 3 times a day.

Bounty

Fugitive Warrant, Wanted Bounty, or Bounty on Global. It has three different stages where Growth Materials such as, skill books, EX-Skill Disks, and materials are obtained.

It can be unlocked after clearing Mission 3 Act 1.

Commission

Special Request or Commissions on Global, are 2 different stages where teachers are able to earn Gold and Exp Report.

It can be unlocked after clearing Mission 1 Act 4.

Scrimmage

School Exchange Meeting or Scrimmage on Global, is a new game mode where teachers are able to earn new Exp materials to level up their student's Unique Equipment.

Total Assault

Total War, Total Assault, or more commonly known as Raid on Global, is game mode where teachers compete for points by battling and defeating the Raid boss. To compete for high score on the leaderboard for various prizes and rewards.

Unlocked after clearing Mission 4 Act 1.

Tactical Challenge

Tactical Competition, Arena, or Tactical Challenge in Global, is a fully automated PvP battle in Blue Archive.

It is unlocked after completing Mission 6 Stage 1.
