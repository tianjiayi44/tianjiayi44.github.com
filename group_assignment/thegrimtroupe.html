The Grim Trope

The Grimm Troupe is the second of the three free content packs planned for Hollow Knight. Its name and content were revealed on September 14th, 2017. It was released on October 26th, 2017.

This update added a major quest with its own characters and bosses, several Charms, and a platforming challenge.

Quests

The Grimm Troupe's Quest is Troupe Master Grimm's request to help the Troupe's Ritual in Hallownest. It has two possible endings, with different rewards, achievements, and characters involved. The two endings are mutually exclusive.
