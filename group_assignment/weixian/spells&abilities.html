The Knight are capable of obtaining several spells and abilities during its journey.

Skill

Spells are activatable after obtaining them either after a boss fight or encounter with a certain NPC in the game. From now on we will refer the spell button as CAST because there are different controls for different devices.

Focus

The main spell that The Knight got right in the beginning of the game. It allows The Knight to heal using soul.

Hold down CAST to activate focus.

Vengeful Spirit

This spell can be obtained after talking to an NPC named Snail Shaman located in Ancestral Mound in the Forgotten Crossroads.

It allows The Knight to launch a projectile in front of it that passes through enemies and deals damage. It also stalls The Knight in the air for a short time and send The Knight in the opposite direction of the force.

Press the CAST button to activate Vengeful Spirit.

Desolate Dive

This spell can be obtained after defeating the boss Soul Master in the City of Tears.

It allows The Knight to strike down to the ground with some SOUL when The Knight was in the air to deal damage around where The Knight land. This spell can also be use to break certain floors that looks fragile. It also provides a small window of invincibility frame during and after the spell has been cast.

Press the DOWN key while in the air and CAST button to activate Desolate Dive.

Howling Wraiths

This spell can be found in the Overgrown Mound within Fog Canyon, next to the entrance to the Queen’s Gardens.

It allows The Knight to release a wave of energy in an arc around and above The Knight, dealing total of three hits. Like Vengeful Spirits this spell can also stalls The Knight in the air but doesn’t send it to the opposite direction of the force.

Press the UP key and CAST button to activate Howling Wraiths.

The spells mention above all have an upgrade of their own to further increase their damage and sometimes the amount of hits dealt to enemies.

Abyss Shriek

An upgraded version of Howling Wraiths which increases its damage, area of affect and hit an additional time. It can be upgraded by standing on a pedestal in a room in the southwest part of the Abyss and use the Howling Wraiths spell.

Shade Soul

An upgraded version of Vengeful Spirit which increases its damage and area of affect. It can be upgraded by requiring the Elegant Key from Sly in Dirtmouth which can open a locked door in Soul Sanctum within the City of Tears. It has been guarded by a strong enemy which require the player to beat before gaining access to Shade Soul.

Descending Dark

An upgraded version of Desolate Dive which increases its damage, area of affect and hit an additional time. It can be upgraded by founding a snail shaman’s corpse encased in crystal in Crystal Peak, within the Crystallised Mound.

Abilities

Abilities are skills The Knight can find throughout the game that make traversing the map easier. From now on we will refer the dash button as DASH, crystal dash as SUPER DASH, jumping as JUMP and using the Dream Nail as DREAM NAIL because there are different controls for different devices.

Mothwing Cloak

Mothwing Cloak

Obtain from a body of a vessel after defeating Hornet in Greenpath in the boss arena. It allows The Knight to dash a short distance in the direction The Knight is facing using the DASH button. It has a short cooldown so that the player can’t dash indefinitely and landing on the ground, bounces off an enemy/object with the Nail or clings to/jumps off a wall using the Mantis Claw refreshes the cooldown.

Shade Cloak

Shade Cloak

An upgraded version of Mothwing Cloak which allow The Knight to dash through enemies and their attacks, as well as Shade Gates. It has a separate cooldown than the original dash which was indicated by shadow particles scattering around the Knight while it is on cooldown, then being absorbed into the Knight when it recharges.  Dashing while the cooldown is in affect will result in an original dash which does not phase through enemies.

Mantis Claw

Mantis Claw

Obtain from a room in Mantis Village. It allows The Knight to cling on walls and jumping off them. It only works on completely vertical surfaces and also used to climb up walls by repeatedly jumping and clinging to the same wall.

Crystal Heart

Crystal Heart

Obtain from an old mining golem at the end of a platform segment in Crystal Peak. Mantis Claw and Mothwing Cloak required. It allows The Knight to fly forward horizontally until colliding with a wall or enemy which will kill it if it has low health. Holding down the SUPER DASH while on the ground or clinging to a wall to concentrate force. Release the button at full charge to blast of.

Monarch Wings

Monarch Wings

Obtain from the Ancient Basin, just after the Broken Vessel boss fight. Crystal Heart required. Allow The Knight to jump again while in mid-air by pressing JUMP while in the air.

Isma’s Tear

Isma’s Tear

Obtain from Isma’s Grove in the Royal Waterways. Crystal Heart and beating Dung Defender required. It allows The Knight to swim in acid without taking damage.

Dream Nail

Dream Nail

Obtain in the Resting Grounds by inspecting the Dreamers Memorial. Doing so sends the Knight into a dream where they receive the Dream Nail from a seer which is a Moth. It is one of the main mechanic of the game.

Hold down DREAM NAIL to charge and swing the Dream Nail. This can perform several functions: Extract Essence from Spirits
Activate Whispering Roots
Collect SOUL from living enemies (33 SOUL)
Challenge Warrior Dreams
Read the minds of NPCs, enemies (excluding Warrior Dreams), and some statues and corpses
Instantly kill both the Shade and Siblings
Summon The Grimm Troupe
Use to set or travel to a Dreamgate (Requires 900 Essence)
Enter the Dream Realm
Challenge Dream Bosses
Access the Shrine of Believers
Enter the minds of Dreamers
Access the White Palace (Awoken Dream Nail, requires 1800 Essence)
Access  Godhome
Obtain Void Heart
Challenge the Radiance
Switch boss forms in the  Hall of Gods

Awoken Dream Nail

Awoken Dream Nail

An upgraded version of Dream Nail after collecting 1800 Essence and returning to the seer. This allows access to dreams and memories that were previously hidden. The Awoken Dream Nail can be used on the corpse of the Kingsmould in the Palace Grounds. This grants entry to the White Palace. Furthermore, the Awoken Dream Nail can be used to view the Dream Nail dialogue of the Shade Cloak creature in the Abyss. This creature has different Dream Nail dialogue depending on whether Void Heart has been obtained.

Dreamgate

Dreamgate

Obtain after collecting 900 Essence and returning to the seer. This allows The Knight to place a Dreamgate at a location, then warp to it from anywhere. Only one Dreamgate can be placed at any time, and placing a new gate removes the previous one. Warping consumes 1 Essence. A Dreamgate can be warped to indefinitely, as long as the Knight has enough Essence.
