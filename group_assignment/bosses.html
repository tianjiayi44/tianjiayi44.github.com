Bosses
Bosses
Bosses are unique enemies in Hollow Knight that typically have higher health or deal more damage than the standard creatures in the same area. The Knight may have to defeat a Boss to access a new area, acquire an item, complete a quest, or simply further the main story of the game. When the Knight encounters a boss, the name usually appears on-screen follow by the music changes into the boss unique battle theme. Counting the variations, there are 47 bosses in the game.
