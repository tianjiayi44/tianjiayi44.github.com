The Knight

The character you play as.

Lore

The Knight has no name according to the developers and so the community usually call it Knight.

The Knight is one of the many discarded vessels. There soul purpose is to contain the Radiance the main villain. There are being made by the Pale King which is the ruler of Hallownest. The Knight is a vessel that unfortunately didn't make the cut to become the vessel to contain the Radiance.

Luckily it found a way to escape it's birthplace and wander out of Hallownest.

The story begin after this incident and the Knight return to Hallownest because of a call by someone either the Radiance or the chosen Vessel.

Health and Soul

Health

Your health in the game is represented by Masks that is shown in the upper-left corner of the screen. You start with 5 and can gain more via collecting Mask Shards. 4 Mask Shards equals to 1 Masks and you can have a total of 9 Masks.

You lost health via taking damage from enemies or from environmental hazards.Health can be regain via Focus or sitting on a Bench.

Lifeblood Masks

Granted by Lifeseeds and certain Charms. These are Masks that is one used and cannot be healed using focus. They also disappear when you are resting on a bench.

Shade

When you die you leave an entity called Shade. You will return to the Bench you last rested on but with a third of your SOUL gauge missing and without any Geo.

In order to regain your whole SOUL and Geo, you need to go back to the place you die and defeat the Shade. For reminder the Shade will fight back and occasionally cast Vengeful Spirit.

SOUL

SOUL is represented by a circular mask to the left of your health. It was used for casting spells and sometimes used for certain Charms.

Soul can be replenished by hitting enemies with your Nail. Gather 3 Vessel Fragments increases your maximum SOUL.
