Nail

The weapon that stays with you till the end

Nail

Nail is the weapon used by several enemies, NPCs, and bosses in Holow Knight, including The Knight.

The Nail is upgradable by the Nailsmith in the City of Tears up to 4 times if given enough Geo and Pale Ore which increase the damage of the Nail.

Upgrades

Old Nail

The original weapon of The Knight.

Damage: 5

Upgrade Cost: none

Sharpened Nail

The second upgrade of the Nail.

Damage: 9

Upgrade Cost: 250 Geo

Channelled Nail

The Third upgrade of the Nail.

Damage: 13

Upgrade Cost: 800 Geo + 1 Pale Ore

Coiled Nail

The Forth upgrade of the Nail.

Damage: 17

Upgrade Cost: 2000 Geo + 2 Pale Ore

Pure Nail

The Fifth upgrade of the Nail.

Damage: 21

Upgrade Cost: 4000 Geo + 3 Pale Ore

Nail Bouncing

By jumping and slashing down with the Nail on environment jugs, spikes, or enemies, the Knight can bounce according to the game's physics which when the Nail hit something, The Knight will bounce back a short distance.

This move, known as "Nail-bouncing" or "pogo-jumping" and is useful for fighting enemies and platforming.

Nail Arts

Nail Arts are different kinds of technique that can be learn through seeking their respective masters. There are 3 different masters which uniquely teaches The Knight their respective technique after paying some geo.
