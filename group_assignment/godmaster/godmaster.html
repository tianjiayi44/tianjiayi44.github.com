Godmaster
Take your place amongst the Gods. New Characters and Quest. New Boss Fights. Epic New Music!
Godmaster is the last of the three free content packs planned for Hollow Knight. It was initially revealed as Gods & Glory along with its content on January 31st, 2018, but renamed to Godmaster on August 15th, 2018 due to a trademark conflict with the mobile game Gods and Glory published by Wargaming. It was released on August 23rd, 2018. This last content pack added an endgame quest and fighting challenges, several characters and secret areas, more Delicate Flower recipients and additional music, along with some remixes.

Pantheons:
Pantheons are a mechanic added to Godhome. They feature a select set of bosses to combat before reaching the "Peak" of each Pantheon and facing the unique final boss of each challenge. Most bosses, with the exception of the final bosses, need to be defeated outside Godhome first before the Pantheon can be accessed that contains those bosses. Completing the first three Pantheons also unlocks Godseeker Mode.
Pantheon of the Master:
Features bosses that can usually be encountered very early on. The final boss is the Brothers Oro & Mato.
Pantheon of the Artist:
This Pantheon contains bosses that can be fought early to mid-game. The final boss is Paintmaster Sheo.
Pantheon of the Sage:
The bosses here are generally found mid to late game. The final boss is Great Nailsage Sly.
Pantheon of the Knight:
This Pantheon is unlocked after the previous three Pantheons have been completed. It has most of the late game bosses, including several which deal two Masks of damage. The final boss is Pure Vessel, a harder and faster version of the Hollow Knight.
